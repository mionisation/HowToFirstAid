Person unconscious & NOT breathing?

  1. 1. Call Ambulance - click here for number

  2. 2. Perform CPR

    1. A) Do 30x chest compressions

      Push HARD about 100 times in a minute. This animation indicates the correct rhytm:
      Click to HEAR rhythm!

      Video by Bangkok Hospital Phuket, Edited by Mikael_Häggström, Creative Commons 3.0 Unported, Link

    2. B) Do 2x emergency breaths

      Tilt head backwards. Hold chin with one hand, pinch nose with the other. Apply mouth to mouth.

      Image by Rama - Own work, CC BY-SA 2.0 fr, Link

    3. C) Repeat Steps A. and B. until ambulance arrives!

Person unconscious & IS breathing?

  1. 1. Call Ambulance - click here for number

  2. 2. Bring in safe distance, away from danger

    Stretch both arms of the person over their head to pull them easily.

  3. 3. Bring in recovery position

    1. A) Place persons nearest arm in right angle on ground
    2. B) Pick OTHER, farer away arm and place palm on opposite cheek
    3. C) Bend up farer away leg, so that foot touches floor
    4. D) Pull this knee towards you so that whole body crouches over
    5. E) Check that airways are free
