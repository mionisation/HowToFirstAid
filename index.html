Person unconscious & NOT breathing?

  1. 1. Call Ambulance - click here for number

  2. 2. Perform CPR

    1. 1. Do 30x chest compressions

    2. 2. Do 2x emergency breaths

    3. 3. Repeat steps 1. and 2. until ambulance arrives

Person unconscious & IS breathing?

  1. 1. Call Ambulance - click here for number

  2. 2. Bring in safe distance

  3. 3. Bring in recovery position

    1. 1. Call Ambulance

    2. 2. Perform CPR
